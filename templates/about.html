Our Mission

Born into a digital world, our generation has never known life without technology, and with, it the ease with which misinformation spreads. At Fake News Inspector, our mission is to help users distinguish between factual and false information. By leveraging machine learning models for natural language processing (NLP), we analyze textual inputs to predict their credibility.

How We Work

Our platform utilizes transformer-based models, such as RoBERTa, fine-tuned on real-world datasets containing both true and false news articles. Through tokenization, preprocessing, and full model fine-tuning, we enable reliable classification of news content. The model is built using the Hugging Face Transformers library and trained with PyTorch.

Why it Matters

Fake news can influence opinions and spread fear. We believe that by providing reliable insights into the news people consume, we can help them reclaim their agency. Whether it’s politics, health, or world events, Fake News Inspector is here to keep you informed with accurate fact checking.
